About BMW

The Ultimate Driving Machine – Since 1916

Legacy of Excellence

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) is one of the world’s most iconic automotive brands. Founded in 1916, BMW has stood for performance, prestige, and innovation. From aircraft engines to cutting-edge electric vehicles, BMW’s journey is a testament to precision engineering.

Every BMW is more than a car — it’s a statement of power, elegance, and driving pleasure.

What Drives BMW

Design

BMW design combines bold styling with aerodynamic precision to create timeless icons.

Technology

iDrive, gesture control, BMW Intelligent Personal Assistant – innovation is at the core.

Performance

From inline-6 to M V8 engines, BMWs are tuned for thrilling dynamics and control.

Sustainability

The BMW i Series leads the shift to electric with zero-emission, luxury EVs.

BMW Timeline

1916

BMW is founded as an aircraft engine manufacturer.

1936

BMW 328 wins major racing events, building the brand’s performance heritage.

1975

BMW launches the 3 Series – its most successful model line.

1994

BMW enters India and becomes a leading luxury car brand.

2023

Launch of BMW i7 and XM – shaping the future of electric luxury.
